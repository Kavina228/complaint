COMPLAINT REGISTRATION


PROCEDURE TO REGISTER THE COMPLAINTS

  1. Register your Complaints by Clicking Complaint Registration tab.

    • Clearly specify the problem

    • Provide the exact location of the systems

    • Remember to note down the ticket number to check the status

    • Please avoid registering the same complaint again

    • For further clarification contact intercom number.

  2. Service Engineer will attend to the calls on the order of registration

  3. Every effort will be made to attend to the calls to resolve at the earliest

  4. Status of your complaint can be checked by entering the Ticket No. on the left top edge of the front page
